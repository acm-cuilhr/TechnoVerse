About

TechnoVerse is a two-day event that aims to bring together the tech enthusiasts from all over the Lahore. The event will be held on 18th and 19th May, 2022 at COMSATS University Islamabad, Lahore Campus. The event will feature a variety of competitions.

F.A.Q

  • Is it possible to register for social nights individually?

    No, you cannot.

  • Are students from off-campus permitted to join TechnoVerse’24?

    Yes, they are welcome.

  • Can off-campus students volunteer for TechnoVerse’24?

    No, volunteering is permitted, but they can participate.

  • Can participants sign up for multiple modules?

    Yes, by registering separately for each module and paying the respective registration fee.

  • Do all modules have identical registration fees?

    Yes, with the exception of the Reels Competition.

  • What is included in the social nights?

    Qawali Night on Day-1 and an Exclusive Dinner on Day-2.
